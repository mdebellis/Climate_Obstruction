The following are prompts for the RAG system to demonstrate its ability to reason and answer questions
using the CSSN Green Washing model. 

Alpro (UK) Ltd is a yogurt and plant- based beverage producer. A recent ad campaign claimed their products were 
"good for the planet" with no other information as to how or why. Is this Green Washing and if so what kind?

Innocent Ltd recently had ads that implied (through imagery and lyrics) that purchasing
their products would have a positive environmental impact when that was not the case. Is this Green Washing?
